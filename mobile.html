ABOUT THE MARG

We Love Margartias

Our family has always associated Tia Linda's margaritas with the great times we have together. These delicious cocktails show up at every family function and now we have made them available for you to enjoy! The margaritas are made with all natural ingredients including organic lemon and lime juice. We wouldn't dare add any artificial coloring or flavoring. These 18% ABV cocktail's taste speak for themselves but watch out because they will sneak up on you! All you have to do is give them a try and you will be hooked on this perfect cocktail, good for any occasion.

All Natural Ingredients:

Organic Lemon & Lime Juice

Real Cane Sugar

Sea Salt

Tequila (of course!)

18% ABV
