ABOUT TIA LINDA

Welcoming, always fun, and definitely the life of the party - our Aunt Linda has always been the one that brings our family and friends together. No matter the event, one thing is for certain, Tia Linda will have a batch of her "famous" margaritas ready for all to enjoy. From holidays to weddings to pool parties and more - these delightful, punch packing cocktails have been part of countless good memories for our family. For years, we have wanted to share this tasty and refreshing recipe with the world and now, with Tia Linda's blessing of course, we finally did it!

So, gather your loved ones, open up a bottle, and start making those memories!

Salud!

"You can't drink all day if you don't start in the morning."

- Tia Linda
